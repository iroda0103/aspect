Submission Guidelines

How to submit your research for ASPECT-2026

Abstract Submission

Submit a 250-500 word abstract outlining your research objectives, methodology, and expected outcomes. All abstracts will undergo peer review.

Review Process

Each submission will be reviewed by at least two experts in the field. Acceptance notifications will be sent within 4 weeks of submission.

Publication

Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings and submitted to Scopus-indexed journals for consideration.

Awards

Outstanding papers will be recognized with awards in various categories including Best Paper, Best Student Paper, and Innovation Award.
