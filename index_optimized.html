Vision & Mission

Pioneering the Next Era of Industrial Science

ASPECT-2026 will be held by Tashkent State Technical University on June 10–11, 2026 — providing a premier international platform for researchers to exchange the latest findings in physical engineering and chemical technology.

This conference aims to provide a forum for sharing the latest research achievements and future development directions. Implementing interdisciplinary research to support national, regional, and international innovative systems in biophysics, computer engineering, engineering management, artificial intelligence, automation, and robotics.

ASPECT-2026 will be indexed in the Scopus database. All authors are cordially invited to submit articles on research achievements.

Call for Papers

Submission Guidelines

Original Research

Submit unpublished research presenting novel contributions to physical engineering and chemical technologies with rigorous methodology.

Format & Length

Full papers: 6–8 pages. Extended abstracts: 3–4 pages. Follow ASPECT template guidelines with specific font and margin requirements.

Review Process

Double-blind peer review by international experts. Decision notification within 4 weeks of submission deadline.

Publication

Accepted papers will be indexed in Scopus and Web of Science. Optional full-text publication in conference proceedings.
