About the Conference

ASPECT-2026 serves as a premier international platform for researchers, engineers, and scientists to exchange breakthrough findings in the convergence of physical sciences and chemical engineering. This year, we emphasize the transformative power of Artificial Intelligence and Automation in optimizing chemical processes and materials synthesis.

Publication & Indexing

All accepted and presented papers will be submitted for indexing in the Scopus and Web of Science databases. High-quality papers will be invited for special issues in Q1/Q2 journals.

Submission Guidelines

  • Use the Springer LNCS Template.
  • Double-blind peer review process.
  • Original research with 90%+ originality.
