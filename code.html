About the Conference

Pioneering the Next Era of Industrial Science

ASPECT-2026 serves as a premier international platform for researchers, engineers, and scientists to exchange breakthrough findings in the convergence of physical sciences and chemical engineering.

This year, we emphasize the transformative power of Artificial Intelligence and Automation in optimizing chemical processes and materials synthesis. Join us in Tashkent to explore how advanced studies are shaping a more sustainable, high-efficiency future.
