Vision & Mission

Pioneering the Next Era of Industrial Science

ASPECT-2026 will be held by Tashkent State Technical University on June 10–11, 2026 — providing a premier international platform for researchers to exchange the latest findings in physical engineering and chemical technology.

This conference aims to provide a forum for sharing the latest research achievements and future development directions. Implementing interdisciplinary research to support national, regional, and international innovative systems in biophysics, computer engineering, engineering management, artificial intelligence, automation, and robotics.

ASPECT-2026 will be indexed in the Scopus database. All authors are cordially invited to submit articles on research achievements.

Call for Papers

Submission Guidelines

Abstract Submission
Submit a 250–500 word abstract outlining your research objectives, methodology, and expected outcomes. All abstracts undergo rigorous peer review by domain experts.
Peer Review Process
Each submission is reviewed by at least two domain experts. Acceptance notifications sent within 4 weeks of submission. Double-blind review process ensures fairness.
Scopus Publication
Accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings and submitted to Scopus-indexed journals for consideration. Proceedings archived in digital libraries.
Awards & Recognition
Outstanding papers recognized with awards: Best Paper, Best Student Paper, and Innovation Award. Award winners invited for extended journal submission.
